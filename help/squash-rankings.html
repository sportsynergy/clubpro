Rankings

Sportsynergy uses a nationally recognized ranking calculation as listed below:
WRO = Winner's old rating
LRO = Loser's old rating
K = constant = .1
D = denominator = .5
PW = Probability that the winner would win = (1/power(10,(-(WRO-LRO)/D))+1))
PL = Probability that the lose would win = (1/Power(10,(-(LRO-WRO)/D))+1))
WRN = Winners's new rating = (WRO+K*(1-PW))
LRN = Loser's new rating = (LRO+K*K(0-PL)

The flames next to player's names indicate that the person has won his/her last match as well as at least 13 games in their last 5 matches.
