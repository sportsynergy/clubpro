Match Types

A court reservation can be made as one of a few possible match types.

A Practice Match is used for those informal games.

When you want to take things up a notch, go with a Challenge Match. A Challenge Match will adjust the players rankings upon recording the match result using a nationally recognized ranking algorithm.

A Solo Match is for when you don't have anyone to play with and more importantly, you don't want anyone to play with.

League Matches will be recorded in the Box League. If you don't see this Match Type in the drop down, that means that your club doesn't have leagues going on.
